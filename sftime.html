Traveler Part 1

Aug 10th 2022 5 Min read


Follow along with me for a bit. Imagine now, regardless if you are in this state or not, you are relaxing somewhere.

Maybe you are reliving or remembering a moment of extreme joy or bliss. A moment etched so clearly in your mind that it offers you comfort.

Comfort, warmth, and even nostalgia when the world seems to want to shake you. Break you.

Now do you feel it? Are you enveloped or feeling that joy or warmth welling inside you? Even if it's a speck, that is enough.

Now continue to imagine. Imagine and feel that joy, happiness, nostalgia, comfort, warmth, and all the other emotions and words that come to ultimately represent human elation.

Now you blink once and open your eyes and suddenly the moment is gone.

You look at your hands and there covered in wrinkles. You try to stand and your back aches and feet hurt.

You get up and walk over to a nearby mirror and you are old now. The years have gone by but you no longer remember those experiences.

You don’t even remember going through them. It seems as though time was taken from you.

Your youth and experiences were taken from you.

You were only allowed to experience them vicariously through scattered images, photos, and broken retellings from those who are still around to remember those times.

You do not remember them completely. You can’t even feel them. Any feelings are gone.

As though those emotions you would or did attached to them were severed.

Your memory is still fine though. You are not senile. You do not have any mental degradation. You can still feel emotions. You are not dull.

Yet those moments you remembered just moments before, those moments, those feelings, are now gone. You can’t get them back.

Time continues.

Now why did I ask you this? Why did I ask you to imagine or remember fond moments? It was for this. So you can know my fear. Having time pass back so fast or having yourself so disconnected from reality or time that you zoom past it all. Any moment good or bad, becomes a moment, a blip, on your life. You do not have any associated feelings with it. You only can categorize it as bad, good, okay but nothing more.
